Welcome!

This is a study for scientific research, created by a team of researchers from Northwestern University and the University of Michigan, United States. The purpose of the study is to gain insight into your understanding of uncertainty in probabilistic social networks. The study has 22 questions, which takes about an hour to complete. Please complete the study in one session using Google Chrome for the best application performance.

You will first be asked to complete a short qualification test. If the system assessed that you passed the test, we will then review some important concepts related to networks and probability before proceeding to the tasks.

All of the information you provide will be used only for scientific study by our research team at Northwestern University and the University of Michigan. The information you provide will remain anonymous in any publications. When you have completed the study, we will ask you to provide your email address and send you a $15 Amazon Gift Card to show our appreciation for your time and effort.

Thank you very much for your participation in this research. Please click "Next" to continue.
